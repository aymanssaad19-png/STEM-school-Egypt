About STEM Schools

STEM Schools in Egypt were established to promote innovation and problem-solving among outstanding students. Through project-based learning, students connect theory with real-world applications, preparing them for future scientific and technological challenges.

Student Projects

  • AI-Powered Speech-to-Text Glasses – Assistive tech for the hearing-impaired.
  • Smart Agriculture System – Automated irrigation using IoT sensors.
  • Water Purification Prototype – Sustainable filtration using natural materials.
